## What it is

**Chompi** is a virtual pet that eats what you eat. You snap a photo of your meal, AI figures out what's on the plate, and Chompi reacts - happy, proud, or a little judgy. The idea is to flip nutrition tracking on its head: you don't log meals for a spreadsheet, you log them because Chompi is hungry.

## Features

- **Camera-first feeding** - point at your plate, AI does the meal analysis
- **A pet with moods** - Chompi reacts to what you feed it and how consistently
- **Streaks, XP and levels** - playful progress instead of guilt-driven charts
- **Wardrobe** - dress up Chompi with items you unlock along the way
- **Meal journal** - your history is there when you want it, never in your face

## The fun part

The hard rule of the project is that the mascot always matters more than the statistics. Every screen starts from "how would a virtual pet game do this?" rather than "what would a fitness app show here?" - which makes even boring things like a history list an interesting design problem.

## What it is

**AirShare** fills a gap Apple never did: AirPlay on Windows. It turns your desktop into an AirPlay source that feels native, fast, and stable - so your HomePods finally work with your PC.

## Features

- **Per-app audio routing** - send Spotify to the HomePod while the game stays on your headphones
- **Built-in equalizer** - band adjustments and presets before the audio leaves your machine
- **Fast output switching** - jump between local speakers and AirPlay targets without fuss
- **Stable playback** - built to run through a full workday, not just a demo
- **Minimal interface** - pair a device and you're done

## Under the hood

The app is built with Tauri - a Rust core doing the audio capture and AirPlay streaming, with a React/TypeScript UI on top. Getting per-app audio capture and the AirPlay 2 protocol to behave on Windows is where most of the fun (and pain) lives.
